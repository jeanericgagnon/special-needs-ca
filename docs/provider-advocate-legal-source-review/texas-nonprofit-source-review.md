*   **Source Name:** Partners Resource Network (PRN)
*   **Source URL:** [https://prntexas.org](https://prntexas.org)
*   **Source Type:** Authoritative Parent Training & Information (PTI) Center
*   **Robots Status:** Allowed
*   **Terms Risk:** Low (Federally funded public service portal)
*   **Should Pull:** Yes

    *   **PATH Project:** ESC Regions 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 (East/Southeast Texas)

*   **Notes:** PRN operates these PTIs. They provide critical ARD/IEP resources.
