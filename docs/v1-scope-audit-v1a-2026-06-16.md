# V1A Scope Audit: Just The Info We Need

Generated: 2026-06-16

## What V1 Actually Is

V1 should be defined narrowly and honestly:

- a truthful, source-backed 50-state disability resource platform
- focused on benefits, waivers, special education, early intervention, forms, appeals, and local support
- built to help families find the right next step without fake completeness

V1 is not yet the full long-term personalized disability navigator.

## Where We Are Now

Current repo evidence shows:

- all 50 states currently pass the generated truth audit as `Public Safe`, `Index Safe`, and `Gold Eligible`
- fake public local assets are no longer detected on county pages
- the public truth model requires valid `source_url`, acceptable `verification_status`, and real contact signal
- the site presents itself as a `50-State Disability Benefits Guide`
- the product already has structured state guidance, core programs, required forms, FAQs, and some saved-plan/dashboard behavior

This means the current product is strong on truthful public directory coverage and much stronger than a thin SEO site.

## What That Does And Does Not Mean

What it means:

- the public trust foundation is real
- the current gold system is working
- the platform is credible as a 50-state disability resource and guidance product

What it does not mean:

- we have all disability information
- every state is exhaustive across every disability use case
- the full personalized navigator is finished
- every local layer is equally deep in practical usefulness

## Honest Product Position

The most accurate current description is:

- we have a truth-first national disability directory and guidance platform
- we do not yet have the full end-to-end recommendation engine

That is the key distinction to protect in product, docs, and marketing.

## Main Gaps To Close

The biggest remaining V1-adjacent gaps are:

- stronger personalized next-step logic
- more normalized entity fields like eligibility, documents needed, timelines, and recommended next action
- clearer freshness and last-verified UX
- consistent parent-facing "what do I do now" guidance across surfaces

## Working Definition Of Success

V1A should be considered successful if we can honestly say:

- every public page is truthful and source-backed
- every state is safe to show and safe to index under the generated rules
- families can find real programs, agencies, forms, and local contacts
- the product gives useful next steps in plain English
- we do not overclaim exhaustiveness or pretend the full navigator is done
