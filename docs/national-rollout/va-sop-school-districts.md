# VA Standard Operating Procedure: School District Verification

**SOP ID:** VA-SOP-01  
**Category:** School Districts & SELPAs  
**Objective:** Verify direct special education contacts for local school districts.

---

## 1. Search Guidelines

1. **Identify Target:** Look up the school district name and state in the queue.
2. **Search Query:** Run a search on Google:  
   `"[District Name] [State]" special education director contact` OR `"[District Name] [State]" student services phone`
3. **Locate Directory:** Open the official district website (ending in `.org` or `.edu`). Locate the "Special Education," "Student Services," or "Exceptional Student Education (ESE)" department page.
4. **Extract Contacts:** Find the name, direct phone extension, and email address of the Special Education Director or Coordinator.

---

## 2. Phone Verification Script

If contact details are ambiguous or not listed on the website:

> *"Hello, my name is [Reviewer Name] from the Special Needs Resource Directory. I am calling to verify the correct public contact number and email for parents who need to request a special education IEP evaluation for their child. Could you please confirm the name and direct phone number of the Special Education Director?"*

---

## 3. Database Logging

- **Verification Status:** Set to `verified` if a direct contact is found.
- **Source URL:** Log the exact URL of the staff directory webpage (do not log the generic district homepage).
- **Outcome:** Enter reviewer initials and the current date.
