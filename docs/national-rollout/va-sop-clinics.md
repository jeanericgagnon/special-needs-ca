# VA Standard Operating Procedure: Children's Hospital & Developmental Clinic Verification

**SOP ID:** VA-SOP-06  
**Category:** Institutional Clinics  
**Objective:** Verify developmental-behavioral pediatrics, autism evaluation, and complex care clinic details.

---

## 1. Search Guidelines

1. **Identify Target Clinic:** Locate major children's hospitals or university developmental-behavioral pediatrics centers in the target state.
2. **Search Query:** Search on Google:
   `"[Hospital Name] developmental pediatrics clinic appointment"`
   `"[University Name] autism evaluation center"`
3. **Verify Scope:**
   - Confirm the clinic specializes in clinical developmental/diagnostic services, not just general pediatric care.
   - Do not scrape general patient portals or scheduling hotlines.
   - Verify the physical address and local direct clinic phone number.
   - Note that physical location is NOT service area. Do not map counties served unless the clinic has an official county-level catchment agreement.

---

## 2. Phone Verification Script

> *"Hello, my name is [Reviewer Name] from the Special Needs Resource Directory. I am verifying the intake coordinator contact for your Developmental-Behavioral Pediatrics (or Autism Diagnostic) clinic. Is [Phone Number] the direct line for scheduling new patient developmental assessments, or is there a specific intake coordinator's line we should publish for parents?"*

---

## 3. Database Logging

- **Verification Status:** Set to `verified` after confirming the specific clinical specialization and scheduling path.
- **Source URL:** Log the exact department homepage or directory listing page.
