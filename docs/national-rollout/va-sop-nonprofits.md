# VA Standard Operating Procedure: Nonprofit Curation & Seeding

**SOP ID:** VA-SOP-03  
**Category:** Nonprofit Organizations  
**Objective:** Verify and seed local parent support groups and regional advocacy organizations.

---

## 1. Curation Guidelines

1. **Verify Mapped Nonprofits:**
   - Open the nonprofit's website. Confirm it provides active services, IEP support groups, parent training, or respite care for children with special needs.
   - Flag any general charity (e.g. general food banks, clothing drives) as `do_not_use` for the special needs directory. We only list disability-focused organizations.
2. **Seeding New Nonprofits:**
   - If a county contains 0 nonprofits, search for regional parent training and information centers (PTIs) or local chapters of The Arc, Autism Society, or Down Syndrome Association serving that area.
   - Enter their office address, phone, website, and the specific conditions they support (e.g., `autism`, `down-syndrome`, `any`).

---

## 2. Database Logging

- **Verification Status:** Set to `verified` once coordinates and special needs focus are confirmed.
- **Source URL:** Log the nonprofit's official "About Us" or "Contact Us" webpage.
