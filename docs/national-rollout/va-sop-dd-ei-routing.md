# VA Standard Operating Procedure: DD/IDD and Early Intervention Routing Verification

**SOP ID:** VA-SOP-05  
**Category:** Developmental Disability (DD/IDD) & Early Intervention (EI) Regional Catchments  
**Objective:** Verify regional catchment boundaries, intake contact details, and routing rules for developmental services and Part C Early Intervention.

---

## 1. Search Guidelines

1. **Identify the State Model:**
   - Regional Center (e.g., California)
   - Local Authority / LIDDA (e.g., Texas)
   - Community Services Board / CSB (e.g., Virginia)
   - County Board of DD (e.g., Ohio)
2. **Search Query:** Search on Google:
   `"[State Name] developmental disabilities regional intake contacts"`
   `"[State Name] early intervention Part C lead agency county directory"`
3. **Map Counties Served:** Ensure each local office maps explicitly to its assigned counties. Do not copy statewide hotlines into local county listings unless the routing is 100% centralized.
4. **Intake Contacts:** Locate the direct intake phone number or email address specifically for new eligibility screenings or early intervention evaluations.

---

## 2. Phone Verification Script

> *"Hello, my name is [Reviewer Name] from the Special Needs Resource Directory. I am verifying the intake routing for the [Agency Name] serving [County Name] County. Is this the correct local intake phone number for families seeking to schedule a developmental disability eligibility screening (or Part C Early Intervention evaluation), or is there a direct department line they should call?"*

---

## 3. Database Logging

- **Verification Status:** Set to `verified` once local intake details and catchment boundaries are checked against official documents.
- **Source URL:** Log the state agency PDF directory or official lookup tool URL.
