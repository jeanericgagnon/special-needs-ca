# VA Standard Operating Procedure: County HHS/DSS Office Verification

**SOP ID:** VA-SOP-02  
**Category:** County Support Offices (Medicaid, Cash Assistance, Food Stamps)  
**Objective:** Verify direct telephone intake numbers for local county welfare and social service offices.

---

## 1. Search Guidelines

1. **Identify Target:** Look up the county office name and state (e.g. Lehigh County CAO, PA).
2. **Search Query:** Search on Google:  
   `"[County Name] County [State]" medicaid assistance office phone`
3. **Locate Directory:** Navigate to the state Department of Health or human services portal (ending in `.gov`). Look for the county assistance office directory map.
4. **Extract Contacts:** Locate the physical building address and the direct local intake phone number for that specific county building (avoid using the statewide `800` customer service hotline unless no local number exists).

---

## 2. Phone Verification Script

> *"Hello, my name is [Reviewer Name] from the Special Needs Resource Directory. I am checking the contact directory for the [County Name] County Assistance Office. Is this the direct local number for parents to check the status of their child's Medicaid application, or is there a different local direct intake number?"*

---

## 3. Database Logging

- **Verification Status:** Set to `verified` if local coordinates are verified.
- **Source URL:** Log the exact URL of the state directory listing for that county.
