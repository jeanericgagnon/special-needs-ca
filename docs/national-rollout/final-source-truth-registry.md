## 2. Source Classification Policy
1. **Verified Official Source:** State agency websites (`.gov`) or direct statewide program portals.
2. **Verified Trusted Nonprofit Source:** Parent Training & Information (PTI) centers, Protection & Advocacy (P&A) organizations, or local chapters of The Arc.
3. **Verified Institutional Source:** University developmental clinics or Children's Hospital developmental-behavioral pediatrics clinics.
4. **Quarantined / Prohibited Source:** Generic homepages too weak, patient portals, staff rosters, or generated fake domains (e.g. `dhhs.[state].gov`).
