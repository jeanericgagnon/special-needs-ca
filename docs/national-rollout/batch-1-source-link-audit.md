# Batch 1 Source-Link Audit

This document records the results of the source link audit conducted on Texas, Florida, and Pennsylvania records.

---

## 1. Audit Summary

We scanned all active records in Batch 1 states to replace weak or generic homepages with deep, localized program links:

* **Texas (TX):** 3,236 database entries audited. Replaced generic `https://hhs.texas.gov` with specific ECI contractor rosters, county board intakes, and LIDDA catchment endpoints.
* **Florida (FL):** 450 database entries audited. Replaced generic APD homepages with direct area office directories and regional Early Steps provider contact maps.
* **Pennsylvania (PA):** 302 database entries audited. Replaced CAO homepages with local assistance office direct telephone locator sub-pages.

## 3. Findings
