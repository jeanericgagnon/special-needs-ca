# North Carolina Resource Truth Map

Authoritative sourcing parameters for North Carolina across all required categories.

## 1. Official Sources & Directories
- **Medicaid Offices (County DSS):** [NCDHHS County Social Services Departments](https://www.ncdhhs.gov/divisions/social-services/local-county-social-services-departments)
- **Developmental Disability (LME/MCO Directory):** [NCDHHS LME/MCO Directory](https://www.ncdhhs.gov/providers/lme-mco-directory)
- **Early Intervention (Part C CDSAs):** [Children's Developmental Services Agencies (CDSAs)](https://www.ncdhhs.gov/divisions/mental-health-developmental-disabilities-and-substance-use-services/child-and-family-mental-health-developmental-disabilities-and-substance-use-services/early-intervention/childrens-developmental-services-agencies-cdsas)
- **Special Education (DPI EC Division):** [NCDPI Exceptional Children Division](https://www.dpi.nc.gov/districts-schools/classroom-resources/exceptional-children)
- **ABLE Savings Program (NC ABLE):** [NC ABLE Program](https://nc.savewithable.com)

## 2. Lead Agencies
- **State Medicaid Agency:** North Carolina Department of Health and Human Services (NCDHHS)
- **Developmental Disability (DD) Agency:** NCDHHS Division of Mental Health, Developmental Disabilities, and Substance Use Services
- **State Education Agency:** North Carolina Department of Public Instruction (NCDPI)
