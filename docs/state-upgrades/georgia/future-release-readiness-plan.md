# Georgia Future Release Readiness Plan

**Target Release Window:** Batch 3  
**Current Gating:** GATED_PILOT (noindex)  
**Date:** June 14, 2026

---

## 1. Release Readiness Roadblocks

Although Georgia has cleared the 40.0% unblocking gate (current rate is **39.52%**), it remains gated due to two core bottlenecks:
1. **School District Gap:** 133 of the 148 school districts in Georgia remain unverified, relying on statewide portals for special education contact information.
2. **County DFCS Offices:** 144 of the 159 county offices are unverified, with no direct telephone numbers for local Medicaid case managers.

---

## 2. Path to Launch Readiness

To bring Georgia's manual review rate below the **5.0% launch threshold**, we must execute the following actions:

* **Phase 1: Priority County Curation (10 hours of VA time)**
  * Manually research and verify the remaining school district directors in the next 15 highest-population counties (e.g. Chatham, Richmond, Bibb).
* **Phase 2: DFCS Office Hotline Audits (15 hours of VA time)**
  * Verify the direct contact lines for local DFCS county offices.
* **Phase 3: Seeding Local Arc Chapters**
  * Seed 5 trusted local chapters of The Arc Georgia to pad out the support cards.
