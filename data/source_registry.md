# 50-State Benefits & Resource Directory: Source Registry Catalog

This registry serves as the authoritative Phase 1 catalog of official portals, Medicaid waivers, developmental disability intake departments, and parent advocacy databases for our initial cohort of states (CA, TX, FL, and NY).

---

## 🐻 California (CA)

### 1. Developmental Disability Services
*   **State Agency**: California Department of Developmental Services (DDS)
    *   **Portal Link**: [https://www.dds.ca.gov](https://www.dds.ca.gov)
*   **Local Entry Point**: Regional Centers (21 catchment boundaries across CA)
    *   **Intake Process**: Contacts vary by local Regional Center catchment. Separated into Early Start (ages 0-3) and Lanterman Act (ages 3+).
    *   **Directory URL**: [https://www.dds.ca.gov/rc/lookup-rcs-by-county/](https://www.dds.ca.gov/rc/lookup-rcs-by-county/)

### 2. Core Medicaid HCBS Waivers
*   **Home and Community-Based Services DD Waiver (HCBS DD)**:
    *   *Target Group*: Individuals diagnosed with intellectual disabilities, cerebral palsy, epilepsy, autism, or other disabling conditions closely related to intellectual disability.
    *   *Waitlist*: None (entitlement under CA Lanterman Act for eligible clients).
*   **Home and Community-Based Alternatives (HCBA) Waiver**:
    *   *Target Group*: Medically fragile children and adults requiring nursing-facility level of care.
    *   *Waitlist*: Long waitlists exist unless qualifying for "Reserve Capacity" priority slots (e.g. under age 21).

### 3. Parent Advocacy & PTI Centers
*   **Parent Training and Information (PTI) Centers**:
    *   *Northern CA*: Rowell Family Empowerment
    *   *Bay Area*: Support for Families of Children with Disabilities
    *   *Southern CA*: Task Parent Training & Information Center
*   **Legal Directory**: Wrightslaw Yellow Pages CA, COPAA Member Directory.

## 🤠 Texas (TX)

### 1. Developmental Disability Services
*   **State Agency**: Texas Health and Human Services Commission (HHSC)
    *   **Portal Link**: [https://hhs.texas.gov](https://hhs.texas.gov)
*   **Local Entry Point**: Local Intellectual and Developmental Disability Authorities (LIDDAs) (39 Local Authorities)
    *   **Intake Process**: Families must contact their local LIDDA directly to apply for services, request assessments, and get placed on waiver interest lists.
    *   **Directory URL**: [https://hhs.texas.gov/services/health/mental-health-substance-use/mental-health-cre-reports/local-intellectual-developmental-disability-authorities-lidda-directory](https://hhs.texas.gov/services/health/mental-health-substance-use/mental-health-cre-reports/local-intellectual-developmental-disability-authorities-lidda-directory)

### 2. Core Medicaid HCBS Waivers
*   **Home and Community-Based Services (HCS) Waiver**:
    *   *Target Group*: Individuals with IDD or related conditions.
    *   *Waitlist*: Extremely long interest lists (often 10-15+ years). Managed directly by local LIDDAs.
*   **Texas Home Living (TxHmL) Waiver**:
    *   *Target Group*: Individuals with IDD living in their own homes or family homes.
    *   *Waitlist*: Managed by local LIDDAs.
*   **Community Living Assistance and Support Services (CLASS) Waiver**:
    *   *Target Group*: Individuals with related conditions (diagnosed before age 22).
    *   *Waitlist*: Centralized state interest list: call 1-877-438-5658.
*   **Medically Dependent Children's Program (MDCP) Waiver**:
    *   *Target Group*: Medically fragile children under age 21 who need nursing-facility level of care.
    *   *Waitlist*: Centralized state interest list: call 1-877-438-5658.

### 3. Parent Advocacy & PTI Centers
*   **Parent Training and Information (PTI) Centers**:
    *   **Partners Resource Network**: [https://prntexas.org](https://prntexas.org) (operates PATH, PEN, and TEAM projects).
    *   **Texas Parent to Parent (TxP2P)**: [https://www.txp2p.org](https://www.txp2p.org).

## ☀️ Florida (FL)

### 1. Developmental Disability Services
*   **State Agency**: Florida Agency for Persons with Disabilities (APD)
    *   **Portal Link**: [https://apd.myflorida.com](https://apd.myflorida.com)
*   **Local Entry Point**: APD Regional Offices (6 regional divisions serving all 67 Florida counties)
    *   **Intake Process**: Applications must be submitted via mail or hand delivery to the APD regional office serving the applicant's county.
    *   **Directory URL**: [https://apd.myflorida.com/region/](https://apd.myflorida.com/region/)

### 2. Core Medicaid HCBS Waivers
*   **iBudget Florida Waiver**:
    *   *Target Group*: Individuals with autism, cerebral palsy, Down syndrome, intellectual disabilities, spina bifida, Prader-Willi syndrome, or children aged 3-5 at high risk of developmental delay.
    *   *Waitlist*: Significant waitlist exists. Crisis waiver enrollment is possible for high-risk situations (e.g. homelessness, danger to self/others).
*   **Consumer Directed Care Plus (CDC+) Waiver**:
    *   *Target Group*: Self-directed option for individuals already enrolled in the iBudget waiver.

### 3. Parent Advocacy & PTI Centers
*   **Parent Training and Information (PTI) Centers**:
    *   **Family Network on Disabilities (FND)**: [https://fndusa.org](https://fndusa.org) (operates statewide programs including POPIN, PEN, and PSN).

## 🗽 New York (NY)

### 1. Developmental Disability Services
*   **State Agency**: NYS Office for People With Developmental Disabilities (OPWDD)
    *   **Portal Link**: [https://opwdd.ny.gov](https://opwdd.ny.gov)
*   **Local Entry Point**: OPWDD Front Door & Developmental Disabilities Regional Offices (DDROs) (5 Regions)
    *   **Intake Process**: Families start by contacting their local Front Door office directly or calling the statewide info line at 1-866-946-9733. Families must complete a Front Door Information Session.
    *   **Directory URL**: [https://opwdd.ny.gov/get-started/front-door](https://opwdd.ny.gov/get-started/front-door)

### 2. Core Medicaid HCBS Waivers
*   **OPWDD Comprehensive HCBS Waiver**:
    *   *Target Group*: Children and adults with developmental disabilities who meet intermediate care facility (ICF/IID) level of care.
    *   *Waitlist*: Moderate wait time. Requires enrollment with a Care Coordination Organization (CCO).

### 3. Parent Advocacy & PTI Centers
*   **Parent Training and Information (PTI) Centers**:
    *   **IncludeNYC**: [https://www.includenyc.org](https://www.includenyc.org) (serving New York City).
    *   **Parent Network of WNY**: [https://parentnetworkwny.org](https://parentnetworkwny.org) (serving Western NY).
    *   **Advocates for Children of New York**: [https://www.advocatesforchildren.org](https://www.advocatesforchildren.org).
